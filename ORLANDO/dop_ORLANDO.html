Details of Participation

Sponsor

Marriott Ownership Resorts, Inc. d/b/a Marriott Vacation Club International

Eligibility

Annual income of $100,000 or more is required. Guest must meet eligibility requirements and be a resident of the District of Columbia or one of the 50 states within the United States. Residents of Maine and Hawaii are not eligible for this offer. Guest may not have attended a sales presentation at any Marriott Vacation Club® property in the last 12 months. Employees of Sponsor and its affiliated companies, and their immediate families, are not eligible for this offer.

Presentation

Attendance at a Marriott Vacation Club sales presentation lasting approximately 90 minutes is required. If married, living together or with significant other, couples must attend the presentation together to receive this offer; individuals not cohabitating may attend separately. Failure to attend the sales presentation will result in the charge of the retail value of the accommodations. This Orlando, Florida, package in a 2-bedroom villa at Marriott’s Cypress Harbour will accommodate up to eight guests and has an average retail value of $690 for stays during April 27, 2019 – December 14, 2019 OR April 27, 2020 – June 30, 2020 (package price of $99) and an average retail value of $955 for stays during December 15, 2019 – April 26, 2020 (package price of $199). Retail values are based on current and projected average room rates during the noted period at the time of print, which are subject to change without notice. Actual retail value is determined by the location’s rack rate for the period of occupancy.

Offer

Package purchase must be made with a major credit card, and a major credit card is required upon arrival and check-in. Accommodations are subject to availability. Airfare, transportation, parking, gratuities, additional expenses and applicable taxes, if any, are not included with this offer. A written confirmation of your reservation is required. One offer per family, no groups. This offer is made on a space-available basis only.This offer is nontransferable, and purchase price is nonrefundable. Offer subject to change without notice.This offer is not valid in conjunction with any other promotion. Failure to show for the reservation without a 30-day notification prior to arrival date will result in the forfeiture of the package purchase price. A $99 fee will apply for any changes to or cancellation of a reservation less than 60 days prior to arrival date. A $99 surcharge will apply to guests staying on their package during any portion of the following holiday periods: President's Day (2/12/20 – 2/17/20), Easter (4/02/20 – 4/13/20), Memorial Day (5/20/20 – 5/24/20), Independence Day (6/26/19 – 7/4/19 and 6/26/20 – 7/4/20), Labor Day (8/28/19 – 9/1/19), Thanksgiving (11/24/19 – 11/30/19), and Christmas/New Year's (12/17/2019 – 1/1/2020), subject to availability. Offer expires December 31, 2019. Travel must be completed within six months of purchase.
